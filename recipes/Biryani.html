Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Description:-

Biryani is a festive mixed rice dish from South Asia, where fragrant, long-grain rice is layered with marinated meat, fish, eggs, or vegetables and aromatic spices. The dish is beloved throughout the region and exists in many regional variations, each with its own distinct flavor profile.
