Butter Paneer

Description:-

Butter paneer, also known as Paneer Butter Masala or Paneer Makhani, is a popular North Indian dish consisting of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy. The name "makhani" comes from the Hindi word for butter, reflecting the generous use of butter and cream that gives the curry its signature velvety texture and mild, slightly sweet flavor.

Ingredients:-

  • Paneer
  • Butter
  • Spices
  • Onions,Tomatoes,Garlic
  • Cream
  • Oil
  • Salt and Sugar

Steps:-

  1. Prepare the base
  2. Make the gravy
  3. Add spices
  4. Enrich the curry
  5. Finish the dish
  6. Serve while it's HOT
