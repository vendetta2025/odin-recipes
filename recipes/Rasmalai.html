Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a classic Indian dessert consisting of soft, spongy cheese dumplings soaked in a sweet, thickened milk, and flavored with cardamom and saffron. The name comes from two Hindi words: "ras," meaning juice or syrup, and "malai," meaning cream. Originating from the Bengal region of the Indian subcontinent, it is a beloved sweet served chilled and garnished with nuts like pistachios and almonds. ALSO ITS MY FAVOURITE SWEET !!

Ingredients:-

  • Cottage cheese (Chenna)
  • Milk
  • Light sugar syrup
  • Saffron

Steps:-

  1. Knead the chenna and Shape them
  2. Prepare the syrup
  3. Boil the dumplings
  4. Cool the dumplings
  5. Prepare the sweetened milk (rabri)
  6. Add dumplings to milk
  7. Chill and serve
